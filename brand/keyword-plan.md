## Business Context
- **Offer:** Premium home cleaning across North Alabama and Middle Tennessee. Recurring + deep + move-out + specialty (post-construction, Airbnb, commercial). Flat-priced from $176 (recurring) and $276 (deep).
- **Audience:** Time-strapped homeowners and renters in 5 markets (Huntsville, Athens, Florence/Shoals, Mountain Brook/Birmingham, Nashville) — plus Airbnb hosts, Realtors, GC superintendents, and small-business operators. High willingness to pay for insured + same-team + flat-quote.
- **Goal:** Leads + organic search authority across the 5 markets. Article 2 of 12 already shipped; this plan defines Articles 3-12.
- **Positioning (per `brand/positioning.md`):** "Booked in 2 hours. Cleaned today. Priced upfront." Differentiation: speed of quote + flat pricing + photo proof + same team every visit. **Do not lead with "49-point checklist" or "veteran + women-owned" in Huntsville or Florence/Shoals** — both collide directly with MaidPro's local franchise positioning.
