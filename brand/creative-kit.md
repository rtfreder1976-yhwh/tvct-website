# Creative Kit — The Valley Clean Team
_Built 2026-05-14 by /creative — read by every creative skill from here forward._

## Brand Colors

The site already commits to a warm, premium, residential palette (deep navy + soft cream + a single accent). The creative kit codifies that into prompt-ready hex values.

- **Primary:** `#1B2A41` "Valley Navy" — deep, calm, premium. Anchors text and dark backgrounds.
- **Secondary:** `#F5EFE6` "Quiet Cream" — soft, off-white, paper-like. Default background for graphics.
- **Accent:** `#C9A86B` "Brass" — restrained gold/brass for arrows, underlines, and the single colored element per slide. Never neon, never bright.
- **Support 1:** `#6B7280` "Linen Grey" — for sub-copy, fine print, dividers.
- **Support 2:** `#2F4F3E` "Sage" — used sparingly for trust badges (insured, 5-star, veteran). Earned, not loud.

## Typography Direction

- **Headlines (display):** Bold modern serif — think Canela, Tiempos, or Fraunkin. High contrast strokes, slightly editorial. **Not** a generic times-roman serif. Conveys quietly premium without shouting.
- **Body:** Clean humanist sans-serif — Inter, Söhne, or a similar low-contrast geometric. Neutral, readable, not corporate.
- **Numerals:** Tabular, large, confident. Numbers do the bragging — "$2M," "2-hour," "49-point," "130+."
- **Hierarchy rule:** One headline per slide. One number per slide. Everything else is supporting.

## Visual Style

- **Photography preference:** Lifestyle-led, warm natural light through east-facing windows, soft shadows. Real-home interiors — kitchen islands, hardwood floors, a single mug, fresh linens — not staging. Slight film grain acceptable.
- **Illustration / graphic style:** Type-led with generous negative space. When a graphic needs a visual element, it's a single photographed object (clipboard, phone screen, key, stopwatch) on the cream background — not vector illustration, not icons-in-circles.
- **Overall mood:** Quietly premium. The Brooklyn-roastery aesthetic, not the franchise-cleaning aesthetic. Confident, neighborly, a little editorial. Reads "you would tell a friend" not "boost this post."
- **Texture:** Paper-stock cream backgrounds (subtle natural-paper grain), satin-finish navy. Avoid glossy, avoid drop shadows, avoid gradients.

### What to avoid (anti-pattern list — becomes prompt guardrails)
- Stock-photo cleaners in identical polos with feather dusters
- Cartoon bubbles, motion-lines, comic effects
- Neon, fluorescent, candy-bright colors
- Aqua/teal "cleaning brand" gradient
- Sparkles, twinkles, magic dust
- All-caps slab headlines stacked with exclamation points
- Photoshopped "before/after" splits with a diagonal line
- Generic "happy customer" smiling-with-arms-crossed shots
- Emoji-heavy graphics
- Three-people-in-a-row team photos against a white wall
- "Trusted by" logo bars when there are no real logos to show

## Logo

- **Path:** `public/icons/icon-clean.png` (site favicon variant) — confirm location with user before pulling. For social graphics in this launch, run **without logo** on slides 1–8 of carousels (keeps the editorial feel) and place a small word-mark only on the final CTA slide.
- **Usage notes:** Word-mark only at small size, bottom-right or bottom-center. Brass color on cream backgrounds, cream on navy backgrounds. Never on top of the photograph.

## Locked style principles (apply to every asset in this campaign)

**Color Treatment:** Cream background (`#F5EFE6`), navy headline (`#1B2A41`), brass accent (`#C9A86B`) for the one colored element (an arrow, an underline, or a number). Never more than three colors per slide.

**Lighting (photo assets):** Soft east-window morning light. 4500K–5200K color temperature. Slight warm shadows. No flash, no studio strobe, no harsh side-light.

**Composition:** Generous margin (8–10% on all sides). One focal point. Type weighted to the upper third or center. White space is the design.

**Mood:** Quietly premium. Confident. Editorial. Reads like a small-batch coffee brand, not a franchise.

**Technical:** Square (1080×1080) for IG carousel slides, 4:5 (1080×1350) for FB hero, 1:1 (1080×1080) for the lead-magnet cover. PNG at export. 72 DPI. Subtle paper grain texture in background.

**Anti-patterns (do not generate):** sparkles, gradient backgrounds, neon colors, cartoon characters, stock-photo cleaners, all-caps headlines stacked, exclamation points in graphics, photoshopped before/after splits, generic team-of-three photos.
