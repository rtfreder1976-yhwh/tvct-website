# Email 1: Delivery

## Subject Line Variants

### A: "The 2-Hour Quote Checklist (inside)" — recommended
Direct, names the asset, matches the opt-in promise. Highest-open subject for delivery emails because it confirms what the reader expected. Parentheses signal "look here."

### B: "Read this before you call any cleaner"
Pattern interrupt + curiosity gap. Doesn't name the magnet, makes them open to find out. Slightly higher open with cold/skeptical lists; slightly higher unsubscribe.

### C: "here's the checklist + a heads-up"
Lowercase, casual, friend-tone. The "+ a heads-up" signals the same-day phone number content below. Best for warm-list subscribers who already know the brand.

**Recommended A/B test:** A vs C
**Reason:** Tests whether your audience prefers the steady, professional tone (matches voice profile) or a more personal/lowercase register. The result informs subject style for emails 2–7.

## Preview Text
"5 minutes to read. Saves you a 5-day quote run-around."

## Send Timing
Day 0 — Immediately after opt-in.
This email has the highest open rate of the entire sequence (~80%+). Any delivery delay erodes the implicit promise on the landing page ("under 60 seconds"). Trigger via ESP automation, not a scheduled send.
