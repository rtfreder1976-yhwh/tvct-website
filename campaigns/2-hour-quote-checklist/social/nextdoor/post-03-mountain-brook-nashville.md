# Nextdoor Post — Mountain Brook / Birmingham AND Nashville

## Why this works
Mountain Brook and Nashville's premium suburbs (Belle Meade, Green Hills, Brentwood, Franklin) skew higher-income and more skeptical of overt sales. The post is even more understated than the Huntsville/Shoals versions, and uses the "estate-grade" supporting trust signal (without making it the lead) since this is the right market for that frame.

Post separately to each Nextdoor neighborhood — same body, swap the "We serve…" line.

Hi neighbors,

I'm Christen — my husband Todd and I run The Valley Clean Team. We serve [NEIGHBORHOOD/CITY], and after enough conversations with new clients about quotes that took a week and prices that doubled on the day, we put together a short checklist for vetting any cleaner — us or otherwise.

→ How to get a flat price in writing instead of a "starting at" quote that climbs
