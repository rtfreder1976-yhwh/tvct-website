# Instagram Reel — "If your cleaner is taking 3 days to quote you…"

## Why this works
Pattern-interrupt opening (literal stopwatch on screen). Hook frame is universal in this category. Reel uses voiceover + on-screen text — no face required, which works for a service brand without an established personal-brand presence.

---

## Visual + script (timeline)

```
0:00–0:02  HOOK
  Visual:   Hand setting a stopwatch on a kitchen counter,
            big numbers visible. Quiet ambient sound only.
  Text overlay (large):
            "If your cleaner is taking 3 days to quote you..."

0:02–0:06  STAKE
  Visual:   B-roll of an empty home, baseboard close-up,
            move-out boxes by a door.
  VO:       "...you've already lost the day you actually
            needed clean."
  Text:     [no overlay this beat]

0:06–0:14  THE FIX
  Visual:   Phone screen showing a text thread —
            "Quote: $381 flat. Arrival window 8–10 AM
             tomorrow." Time stamp 47 minutes after
             a quote request.
  VO:       "A real local team replies in under two hours,
            with a flat number, and a same-day window in
            writing. That's the standard."
  Text:     "Quote in under 2 hours · Same-day arrival
             · Flat price · In writing"
            (one line at a time, beat with the VO)

0:14–0:20  PROOF / BRAND
  Visual:   Quick cut to a TVCT before-and-after — shiny
            stovetop or a streak-free window.
  VO:       "We're The Valley Clean Team. Huntsville,
            Athens, the Shoals, Birmingham, Mountain Brook,
            Nashville. Same team every visit."
  Text:     "Same team, every time"

0:20–0:22  CTA
  Visual:   End card: lead-magnet PDF cover graphic with
            "Free 7-question checklist."
  VO:       "Free checklist link is in our bio."
  Text:     "Link in bio · or DM 'checklist'"
```
