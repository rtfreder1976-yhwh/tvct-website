## Cross-platform notes

- **Pin the Instagram carousel-01** to the top of the profile for the duration of the campaign (4–6 weeks). It's the highest-saving piece.
- **All Facebook posts** should be linked to the same landing page URL with UTM tracking — `?utm_source=facebook&utm_medium=organic&utm_campaign=2hr-checklist&utm_content=[post-name]`. Same UTM pattern for IG (link in bio with UTM-tagged URL), Nextdoor (UTM in the comment-1 link), LinkedIn (UTM in comment-1 link).
- **Nextdoor links go in the first comment**, not the body. Posts with body links get suppressed.
- **LinkedIn posts come from Todd's personal profile**, NOT the business page. Personal-profile reach in 2026 is 3–5x higher.
- **Instagram reels skip TVCT face/voice** — there's no established personal-brand on the brand's IG, so VO-only with on-screen text outperforms a face-on-camera attempt at this stage.
- **No Facebook Reels** in this batch. Service-business Facebook Reels in 2026 reach but don't convert. Run static Facebook posts for opt-in, save Reel-format for Instagram.
- **Skipped platforms and why:** Twitter/X (zero local-service signal in TVCT's 5 markets), TikTok (would require sustained creator presence — not the lift to take on with one campaign), YouTube/Shorts (production lift outweighs return for a checklist promo), Threads (too small in this region for service businesses), Bluesky (wrong audience entirely), Reddit (no fit subreddits with enough local concentration; r/Huntsville is the only candidate and self-promotion rules apply).
