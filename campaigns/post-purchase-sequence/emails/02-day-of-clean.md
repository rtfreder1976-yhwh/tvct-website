# Email 2: Day of Clean

## Subject Line Variants

### A: "We're on the way today (and a quick favor)" — recommended
Curiosity + utility. The parenthetical sets up the review ask later without being pushy.

### B: "Your team arrives in your window today"
Logistical, neutral. Better if Christen-1 was warm enough that customers don't need more warmth.

### C: "clean day"
Two words, lowercase. Maximally personal. Best for engaged repeat readers.
