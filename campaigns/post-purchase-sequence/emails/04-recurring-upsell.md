# Email 4: Recurring Upsell

## Subject Line Variants

### A: "Most customers book us again around now" — recommended
Social proof + timing. Implies a pattern the reader is part of. Highest open + click rate for this type of email.

### B: "14 days in — what most customers do next"
Specific number, slightly less warm. Better if email 3 was opened but not replied to.

### C: "the recurring question (no pressure)"
Acknowledges the ask. Best for engaged subscribers.

## Preview Text
"Bi-weekly is the right answer for ~70% of our customers. Here's how to tell if you're one of them."

## Send Timing
**14 days after the clean, 8 AM CT.** This is the moment most homes start showing the wear that a recurring schedule prevents. Earlier and it feels pushy; later and the bounce-out is too high.

---

## Email Copy

Hi {{first_name}},

It's been two weeks since we were at your place. About now is when most of our customers either:

a) Book us again because they want to keep the home in the state it was in on clean day, or
b) Don't, and call us 4–6 months later when the home is back to where it was before we cleaned it.

Both are fine. But the math is interesting enough that I want to lay it out.

**The math:**

Most customers fall into bi-weekly (every other week). For a 3-bed, 2-bath, ~2,000 sqft home, that runs around **$221 per visit** with the bi-weekly discount.

If you did the same thing as one-off cleans you'd pay full deep-clean rate ($260+) every visit AND need to schedule, vet, and wait every time. The per-clean savings is ~$39. More importantly, recurring customers don't pay for the deep-clean reset every six months because the deep work never piles up.

The pattern across our recurring customers:

→ Their home stays consistently in the state it was on clean day
→ They stop thinking about cleaning between visits
→ The same crew shows up, learns the home, and the cleans get better over time
→ They keep the post-clean photos coming for proof
→ If they ever need to skip a visit (vacation, holiday week), they just text us — no fee

**Our recurring rates:**

| Frequency | Per visit | Discount |
|---|---|---|
| Weekly | from $99 | 20% off |
| Bi-weekly | from $135 | 15% off |
| Monthly | from $160 | 10% off |

Same team every visit. $2M insured. Photo proof every time. Cancel anytime — no contract, no fees.

**This is for you if:**

- You'd rather not think about house cleaning between visits
- You like that the same person will learn your home and the things that matter to you
- You'd rather pay a known monthly rate than re-quote each time

**This isn't for you if:**

- You only need a clean once or twice a year — that's a totally fair strategy
- You're out of the home most of the time (Airbnb hosts, snowbirds) — different sequence for that

If recurring sounds right, the easy way is to call or text and we'll set up the schedule in one conversation:

If now isn't the time, no problem. You'll stay on our standing list — we send something useful every couple of weeks, and you can unsubscribe any time.
