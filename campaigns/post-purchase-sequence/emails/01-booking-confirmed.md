# Email 1: Booking Confirmed

## Subject Line Variants

### A: "You're on the schedule — here's what happens next" — recommended
Confirms the booking is real, signals what's coming. Friendly, low-stakes. Matches the steady voice.

### B: "Booking confirmed: {{appointment_date}} at {{appointment_time}}"
System-feel confirmation. Better if your audience is more rational than warm.

### C: "the small things to know before clean day"
Curiosity-led, lowercase. Best for warm leads who already trust you.

## Preview Text
"A short note about clean day, the photos, and what to do with the dog."

## Send Timing
Within 1 hour of booking. The faster this lands, the lower the cancellation rate. Trigger via GHL workflow "Cleaning Booked" path; do NOT schedule for a specific time of day.

---

## Email Copy

Hi {{first_name}},

You're on the schedule for {{appointment_date}}. Here's everything you need to know — it's shorter than you'd think.

**What to expect on clean day:**

- The same team will arrive within your 2-hour window. You'll get a text 30 minutes before they're at your door.
- The clean takes 3–6 hours depending on the size and condition of the home. We'll text when we start and when we finish.
- We bring all supplies and equipment. You don't have to prep anything.
- We use pH-neutral, professional-grade products that are safe for kids, pets, and original hardwoods.

**Three small things that help us help you:**

1. **Pets** — if anyone in the home doesn't love strangers, set them up in a quiet room with food and water. The team is trained to leave that door closed.
2. **Valuables** — if there's something irreplaceable (jewelry, an heirloom, a stack of cash), tuck it in a drawer. Everyone we send is background-checked and bonded, but the rule we live by is: don't put a temptation in front of a stranger.
3. **Anything off-limits** — a room you don't want touched, a delicate surface that needs special care — text us at the number below before clean day and we'll note it in the file.

**The photos.**

After the clean is done, we send you photos of every room. Inside the oven if it was on scope, behind the toilet, baseboards in the empty rooms. It's so you don't have to be there to verify, and so you have something concrete if anything got missed.

That's it. Anything else, just hit reply — I read these.
