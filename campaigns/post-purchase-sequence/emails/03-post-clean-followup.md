# Email 3: Post-Clean Follow-up

## Subject Line Variants

### A: "How did we do? (and your photo proof, attached)" — recommended
Asks the question. Confirms the photos are in the email. Highest open of any post-clean follow-up format we've tested.

### B: "Yesterday's clean — yes or no?"
Direct, binary. Higher reply rate, slightly lower open. Good for second-time customers.

### C: "your photos from yesterday + a yes/no question"
Lowercase, personal. Best for warm subscribers who've engaged with prior emails.

## Preview Text
"Two-minute answer. Honest one is the most useful."

## Send Timing
**8:00 AM CT the day after the clean.** Window: when they're checking morning email but before the day gets busy. Don't send the same day — they need a night to look around.
