About The Valley Clean Team

A family-owned business built on trust, dedication, and a commitment to making your space shine

Our Story

The Valley Clean Team was founded with a simple mission: to provide professional, reliable cleaning services that exceed expectations. What started as a family-owned business has grown into a trusted name across Alabama and Tennessee.

For over three years, we've been serving our community with pride. We're not just about cleaning homes and businesses – we're about building relationships, earning trust, and becoming part of the communities we serve.

As a veteran-owned business, we bring military precision, discipline, and dedication to every job. We understand the value of hard work, attention to detail, and commitment to excellence.
