# Funnel Diagnosis — Search Console + Google Business Profile

_Generated 2026-06-07 from Google Search Console performance (Mar 6 – Jun 5, 2026,
92 days) + the Google Business Profile summary. This is the lead/demand data
BookingKoala couldn't provide — it resolves the demand-vs-conversion question._

## The question this answers
Earlier analysis showed 2026 doing equal revenue on ~32% fewer jobs (premium
repositioning working) — but we couldn't tell whether the lower job count meant
**fewer leads (demand problem)** or **deliberate higher-value filtering**. Now we can.

## The verdict: NOT a demand problem. It's a RANKING problem.

**Search demand is healthy and growing:**
- Impressions rising: ~1,540/day (Apr) → ~2,080/day (May) → ~2,720/day (early Jun).
- ~43,000 impressions/month. Google is showing TVCT to *more* people over time.
- Clicks steady-to-rising (~11/day → ~15/day).

**But the site is stuck on page 2 and never reaches page 1:**
- Average position **~17**, and **0 of 92 days** ranked on page 1 (position ≤10).
- Range 12.5–22.5. Slightly *worsening* (16.5 → 17.5 across the window).
- Consistent with the ~May 2026 site relaunch still climbing out of re-indexing
  (`PROJECT_CONTEXT.md`).

**At position 17, the 0.62% CTR is normal — the problem is the position, not the listing.**

## The opportunity (the highest-leverage number in the business)

| Scenario | Monthly organic clicks |
|---|---|
| Today (~43K impr × 0.62% CTR @ pos 17) | **~266** |
| If ranking reached page 1 (~3.5% CTR) | **~1,500 (5.6×)** |

That 5.6× more traffic pours into a funnel that in 2026 converts at **$365 AOV, 68%
recurring**. Fixing rank is the growth thesis: high-intent demand already exists; it's
just not being harvested.

## Google Business Profile (the period you shared)
- **1,597 profile views** → ~266 actions (**53 calls + 5 chats + 208 website clicks**) ≈ 17% view→action.
- Found ~50% Google Search mobile, 29% desktop, 17% Maps desktop, 5% Maps mobile.
- Search terms are mostly **generic + geo** ("cleaning service florence al", "house
  cleaners near me", "cleaning services in alabama") — i.e., money terms, all showing
  **<15 impressions each**. Lots of long-tail geo coverage, none of it ranking high.

## What this reorders in the plan
The whole picture is now coherent and encouraging:
1. **Premium repositioning is working** (revenue held on fewer, higher-value jobs).
2. **Demand exists and is growing** (~43K impr/mo).
3. **The bottleneck is page-2 ranking** — the most fixable kind of problem.

**New #1 strategic priority: get money pages onto page 1.** This outranks new lead-gen
channels, because you already have 43K impressions/month of demand you're not capturing.
Channel diversification (the old "single-channel Google risk") matters long-term, but the
*immediate* 5.6× is in ranking the demand you already have.

## Caveats
- Search Console window is 92 days (Mar–Jun 2026); no older baseline here to see the
  relaunch dip directly, but position trend + relaunch timing line up.
- "Position 17 avg" is blended across all queries; some long-tail geo terms rank better
  than money terms. A query-level export (top pages/queries by position) would pinpoint
  WHICH money pages are closest to page 1 — the fastest wins. That's the next data pull.
