## AEO & "Elevated Direct Response" Requirements for Every Post
1.  **AEO Answer Block:** Immediately following the H1, include a direct 2-3 sentence summary that answers the question clearly to capture AI search visibility.
2.  **Definition Sections:** Use "What is [Term]?" formatting to capture rich snippets.
3.  **Local Context:** Weave the local market name (e.g., "Here in Limestone County...") organically into the body content.
4.  **Internal Linking:** Link to the relevant location Hub page, a primary Service page, and at least one other blog post.
5.  **Direct CTA:** End with a strong "Elevated Direct Response" CTA offering transparent action (e.g., "See our transparent pricing and book your Huntsville home cleaning today at...).
