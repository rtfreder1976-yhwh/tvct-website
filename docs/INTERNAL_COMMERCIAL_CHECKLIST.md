# 📝 Internal Checklist: Commercial Facility Walkthrough

This checklist serves as the operational standard for Todd, Christen, and the TVCT team when performing initial site visits for a commercial quote.

## 1. Pre-Walkthrough Prep
- [ ] Confirm the facility type (Medical, Dental, Church, Office).
- [ ] Review any specific notes from the quote request.
- [ ] Bring: Tape measure, flashlight, clipboard/tablet, business cards.

## 2. The Site Visit (Walkthrough)

### High-Frequency Areas
- [ ] **Restrooms**: Check for dispensers, tile condition, and hidden areas (behind pipes).
- [ ] **Breakrooms/Kitchens**: Note appliances, surface types (granite vs. laminate).
- [ ] **Glass/Entryways**: Determine frequency for fingerprint/smudge removal.

### Specialized Areas (Medical/Dental)
- [ ] **Exam Rooms**: Identify bio-hazardous disposal points (we clean around these, not inside them).
- [ ] **Wait Areas**: Check fabric vs. hard surface upholstery.
- [ ] **Air Flow**: Check vents and intake grilles for visible dust.

### Sanctuary/Large Space (Churches)
- [ ] **Flooring**: Type of carpet vs. hard floor. Square footage estimate.
- [ ] **Seating**: Note any specialized cleaning needs for pews/chairs.

## 3. Operations & Logistics
- [ ] **Access**: Lockbox, key, or alarm code?
- [ ] **Time Window**: When can the crew enter? (Preferred after-hours info).
- [ ] **Water/Supply Access**: Where is the mop sink and supply closet?
- [ ] **Trash Disposal**: Where is the dumpster located?

## 4. Safety & Compliance
- [ ] **OSHA Ready**: Note any visible safety hazards for the crew.
- [ ] **SDS**: Where will the Safety Data Sheets be stored on-site?

## 5. Follow-Up
- [ ] Record the estimated weekly man-hours.
- [ ] Send the finalized PDF proposal via GHL within 24 hours.
