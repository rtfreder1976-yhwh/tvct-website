# Review Engineering Templates
**The Valley Clean Team**

Use these templates to guide happy customers toward keyword-rich reviews that boost local SEO.

---

## How to Use These Templates

1. **After service completion**, send via SMS (GHL automation) or text manually
2. **Include the direct Google review link** in every message
3. **Rotate templates** to get variety in review keywords
4. **Personalize** with customer name and service type when possible

**Google Review Link:** `https://g.page/r/CQ3kKbGJZuEaEBM/review`

## Keyword Phrases to Encourage in Reviews

Coach customers to naturally include these phrases when appropriate:

### Location Keywords (High Value)
- "cleaning service in [Huntsville/Florence/Athens/etc.]"
- "[City] house cleaning"
- "maid service [City] AL"
- "best cleaning company in [City]"

### Service Keywords
- "deep cleaning"
- "move-out cleaning" / "move-in cleaning"
- "recurring cleaning" / "weekly cleaning"
- "same-day cleaning"
- "post-construction cleaning"

### Trust/Quality Keywords
- "pet-safe cleaning"
- "same team every time"
- "on time"
- "thorough"
- "trustworthy"
- "professional"
- "attention to detail"
- "veteran-owned"

### Emotional Keywords (Google loves these)
- "highly recommend"
- "exceeded expectations"
- "life-changing"
- "stress-free"
- "peace of mind"
